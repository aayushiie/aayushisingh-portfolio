Aayushi Singh

It was in 2012 when I decided that I love technology. Currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science, I'm still amazed by the world of computers. I enjoy experimenting in development, creative coding, and designing. I have a blog where I primarily document my learning journey in the context of computer science.

I also love writing poems and personal essays, often inspired by my journal named "warped window". I worked as a personal essay writer for over a year at a digital magazine called Outlander.

I am passionate about reading and learning from books. In July 2024, I started a reading club on Instagram to promote awareness of social issues and diversity in reading. The aim is to introduce short-form compositions to traditional book clubs, featuring a diverse range of writers.

I'm also a fan of 80s indie music and love going on long walks, especially after a deep cleaning session.
