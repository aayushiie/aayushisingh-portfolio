WhatsApp Fact-Checker

Category

UI/UX

Role

UX Researcher | UI Designer

Tools

Figma | Adobe CC
view on figma

WhatsApp is a popular messaging platform used by millions worldwide. With its growing user base, there is an equivalent rise of fake news and misinformation. This project aims at verifying the authenticity of news that is shared on the platform by integrating an AI-powered fact-checking tool.
