Digital Art

The Orange

The Orange by Wendy Cope is a poem that emphasizes the significance of relationships and finding joy in everyday life. It conveys that true happiness comes from being alive and sharing moments with the people we care about. I created this artwork [not an original design] using oil pastel brushes to capture that emotion and highlight the vibrant, uplifting essence of these connections. The text at the top are the last two lines of the poem.

Tools: Gimp
